Roasted Butternut Squash Seeds

Don't throw away your butternut squash seeds; roast them instead! Roasted butternut squash seeds make a great snack or salad topper.

  • 1/4 cup butternut squash seeds, rinsed and patted dry
  • 1/2 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
  1. Preheat oven to 325Â°F. Toss squash seeds, oil and salt together on a large rimmed baking sheet; spread in a single layer. Roast, stirring halfway through, until the seeds start to pop and are lightly browned, 14 to 15 minutes. Let cool on the baking sheet for 10 minutes.
