Strawberry Cucumber Juice

This refreshing, healthy strawberry-cucumber juice is also made with an apple and carrots, making it taste like the farmers' market in a glass.

  • 6 fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 large red apple, cut into eighths
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled
  • Ice
  1. Working in this order process strawberries, cucumber, apple and carrots through a juicer according to the manufacturer's directions.
  2. Fill 2 glasses with ice, if desired, and pour the juice into the glasses. Serve immediately.
