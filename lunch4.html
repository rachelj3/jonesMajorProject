Avocado Pizza

Avocado Pizza on a crispy puff pastry crust with fresh tomatoes, avocado chunks, and the most delicious sauce!

  • Pesto Pizza Ingredients
  • 3 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp honey
  • 1/8 tsp tumeric
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 cup chopped cashews
  • 2/3 cup fresh cilantro
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 green onions
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Sauce Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 green onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • A squeeze of fresh lemon juice
  • Pizza Topping Ingredients
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 2 medium tomatos
  • 1-2 firm ripe avocados
  • 1/2 medium red onion
  1. Pizza Pesto: Combine all ingredients in a food processor and pulse until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.
  2. Avocado Sauce: Combine all ingredients in a food processor and pulse until smooth. You may need to stop the food processor and scrape the sides to blend all ingredients well.
  3. Assembling Pizza: Preheat the oven to 400F.
  4. Roll out puff pastry on a floured surface 1/4â€³ thick (it does not have to be perfectly round). Pierce the puff pasty with a fork to prevent bubbling and transfer to a parchment lined baking sheet or pizza stone.
  5. Spread the pizza pesto evenly over the puff pasty and sprinkle with cheese. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes or until golden crisp around the edges.
  6. Remove the puff pasty from the oven and set aside to cool slightly. Then layer on the diced tomatoes, avocado, and red onions.
