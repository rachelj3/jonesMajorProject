Kale Salad with Lemon Dressing

Mouthwatering kale salad recipe with zesty lemon dressing, chickpeas, avocado, Parmesan, and nuts. It's sweet, savory, tender, and crunchy!

  • 1/2 pound Kale
  • 1 lemon, zest and juiced
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, grated
  • 1/8 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, rinses and drained
  • 1/2 large avocado, pitted and diced
  1. To prepare KALE, rinse, pat dry, and chop or tear into pieces.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together LEMON JUICE, LEMON ZEST, MAPLE SYRUP, DIJON, GARLIC, SALT, and PEPPER until well-combined. Slowly drizzle in OLIVE OIL, whisking continuously to create a creamy dressing. Cover and refrigerate.
  3. To serve, pour dressing over kale and firmly massage a few minutes until the leaves soften and all surfaces are well-coated.
  4. Add recommended mix-ins including CHICKPEAS, AVOCADO, along with any other desired ingredients. Toss and serve.
